Runalith Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Runalith is a block puzzle game published by AlisahinDev. This policy explains what information the app uses and how third-party services may handle data when you play.

Information Runalith Stores

Runalith stores game progress, settings, scores, achievements, unlocked themes, daily challenge state, and ad reward limits locally on your device. This data is used to keep the game working between sessions.

Runalith does not require an account and does not ask you to submit your name, email address, contacts, photos, precise location, or payment information.

Advertising

Runalith uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect and share data such as IP address, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, product interactions, ad views, app launch information, and diagnostic information for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, and service operation.

Google describes its advertising data practices in its own policies and SDK disclosures: Google Privacy Policy, Google Play data disclosure for AdMob, and App Store data disclosure for AdMob.

Rewarded ads are optional. Interstitial and banner ads are only used in non-daily game flows so the Daily Challenge remains fair.

Children

Runalith is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service, contact us so we can review the issue.

Data Security

Local game data stays on your device unless platform services or ad SDKs process data as described above. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, but Runalith keeps its own data collection minimal.

Changes

This policy may be updated when Runalith changes features, SDKs, or store requirements. The updated policy will be published on this page.
